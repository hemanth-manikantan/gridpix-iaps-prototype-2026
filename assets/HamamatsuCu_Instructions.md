# Switch on instructions for Hamamatsu Head-on Tube
- Switch on Grid G2 +100 V (HP6186C)
- Switch on Multi-meter
- Switch on G1 -60 V (Elind)
- Switch on 12 V (Thermionic heater). Power supply name: Electronic System
- Switch on HV = 12 kV (Fug), I = 0. Rise the voltage to 12 kV (Cu), 6 kV (Ca), 15 kV (W).
- Switch Fug display to current (Limits for Ca 20 uA, Cu 200 uA, and W 200 uA)
- Regulate G1 to get desired flux (max 0 V, for Cu this is about -20 V). Monitor Fug that it does not exceed the current limits for Anode.
- Data acquired at -20.4 V.
