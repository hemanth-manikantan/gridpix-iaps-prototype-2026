**Configuration/Motivation**
- W30-F11 1 cm 1.2 bar
- Set Grid at 430 V (best EnRes as per central illumination data with 55Fe and 109Cd)
- Set Drift Field at 1000 V/cm (going with simulations for diffusion minimum)
- Max allowed voltage difference between Anode and Grid to prevent sparks (info from Vlad) is (10% of Drift Field)+20%
- For 1000 V/cm, this sets the max allowed voltage difference Grid-Anode to 100+20 = 120 V
- First run with a coarse step of 30 V
 - Scan for Anode-Grid Voltages with Grid fixed and establish the best Anode voltage based on Stokes Maps
